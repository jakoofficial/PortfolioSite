About me

Who am i?

I am a student in Denmark studying to become a programmer, currently working in C# and GDscript making various projects.

I have been making software and games since the age of 10, where I learned of a program called GameMaker. This program gave me the initial push in my future direction of programming.

While working on my first game in GameMaker I had no idea that the engine had scripting, but as I learned through watching tutorials and reading about the engine, I switched to the in engine language GML (GameMaker Language) and was feeling like a programmer for the first time.

Now I mainly use the OpenSource game engine Godot as my primary game making tool. This engine uses a language similar in structure to Python and is easy to learn but still powerful for experienced users. The engine does support C# aswell.

I studied to become a web-developer for 2 years at Roskilde Tekniske Skole but ended up switching my focus after graduating to put more strength into studying software development in C# and game design at HTX in Ringsted.

After graduating from HTX I took some time to learn by doing and working on smaller projects that I then published to the website Itch.io where few are still up to play/download.

After a one year break from school I decided that I wanted to continue my studies in programming, so I moved to Aalborg where I now study at Tech College. While going here I have made a small note taking program that is up on my itch.io page.

In my free time right now, I am working on small games in the Godot engine. One is a small horror game and another is a custom Command Prompt with custom commands made from scratch.

If you want to see my old and newer projects you can see them under the Projects tab.

Thank you for reading.

- Jacob Ø. Andersen
